About this App (Context)

What are Runes? In Elden Ring, "Runes" are the currency used to purchase items and level up your character (similar to XP).

How does the Math work? The cost to level up is not linear. It follows a specific game formula:
Cost = ((x+0.1) * ((Level+81)^2)) + 1.
As you get stronger, the cost grows exponentially.

Is it broken? No! If you test Level 1 to Level 2, the result will be 673 Runes, which matches the official game data exactly.
